Section 16

PbD in Operation

Innovative Privacy by Design applications and practices abound. Many use cases are described and showcased at www.PrivacybyDesign.ca. These include: whole body image security scanners that detect aviation threats but not the identities of passengers; biometrics systems that identify only casino patrons who have voluntarily enrolled; interactive digital signage that targets information and ads based on anonymous viewers' age and gender; privacy-enhanced road tolling and in-home smart metering and remote-home care applications, and enterprise- wide information systems that ensure effective oversight and accountability. PbD encourages the raising of privacy benchmarks, the setting of new privacy practice standards, and open competition on the basis of strong privacy features.
