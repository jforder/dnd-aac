13)PbD Foundational Principle: Visibility and Transparency – Keep it Open

Privacy by Design seeks to assure all stakeholders that whatever the business practice or technology involved, it is in fact, operating according to the stated promises and objectives, subject to independent verification. Its component parts and operations remain visible and transparent, to both users and providers alike, in order to satisfy customers, business partners, regulators, and shareholders. Visibility and transparency are essential to establishing accountability and trust.
